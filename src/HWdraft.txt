## as a roleplaying game
Heavy Wizardry experiments with blurring the lines of what is traditionally considered in-game and outside of it. By expanding this "ludological magic circle", I hope for it to encourage the the player/viewer to contemplate real-life issues analogous to those presented in the text. 

## as an art book
As the game itself is about breaking rules, so the art direction breaks with established rules of “proper” graphic design. A big focus of this project is its harsh, experimental art style that reflects the sinister aspects of technology that the game itself explores.
